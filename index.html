Group 8: KunMindTheGap - Yi Ethnic Group

We are a group of NTU-USP students from diverse disciplines, including business, arts, computing, and psychology, exploring the cultural heritage of the Yi ethnic group. As part of our research, we traveled to Kunming, China, for six days to collect qualitative data through interviews and fieldwork. Our focus is on traditional Yi costume embroidery, its symbolism, and the impact of modernisation on these techniques.

This website serves as a media deliverable to showcase our findings and insights, highlighting the evolving relationship between tradition and modernity in Yi craftsmanship.
